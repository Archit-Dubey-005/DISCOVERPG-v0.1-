Our Story

It started with a simple problem.

In 2020, our founders—themselves recent college graduates—watched a friend struggle to find decent accommodation near their university. After visiting 12 cramped, overpriced rooms in unsafe neighborhoods, they settled for something far below their expectations simply because exams were approaching.

That should never happen.

What began as a shared frustration became a mission. We realized thousands of students across the country faced the same nightmare—scrambling for last-minute accommodations while juggling academics, often falling prey to unverified listings, hidden costs, and unsafe living conditions.

So we built DiscoverPG.

We started by personally visiting properties near major universities, verifying every detail, and building relationships with trustworthy property owners. Today, we've grown into a platform that connects students with quality PG accommodations, but our core belief remains unchanged: every student deserves a place they can call home.
